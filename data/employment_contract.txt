EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

SECTION 1: EMPLOYMENT TERMS

The Employee shall commence employment on January 1, 2026. The first six months shall be considered a probation period. During probation, performance reviews shall be conducted monthly by the reporting manager.

SECTION 2: NOTICE PERIOD

Either party may terminate employment by providing ninety (90) days written notice. The Company may waive part or all of the notice period at its discretion.

SECTION 3: WORK HOURS

The Employee shall work forty (40) hours per week. Standard working hours are from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday.

SECTION 4: LEAVE POLICY

The Employee shall be entitled to twenty (20) days of annual paid leave each calendar year. Unused leave may be carried forward for one year.

SECTION 5: HEALTH BENEFITS

The Company shall provide health insurance coverage for all full-time employees. Dependent coverage is available for spouses and children under twenty-one years of age.

SECTION 6: TERMINATION

Violation of Company policies, fraud, misconduct, or repeated failure to perform assigned duties may result in termination of employment.
